## **2. DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS - SEPP HOUSING 2021**

### **2.1 Principal Dwelling Standards**
- [ ] Site area complies with LEP minimum
- [ ] Building height ≤ 9m (unless LEP specifies otherwise)
- [ ] Maximum 2 storeys at boundary
- [ ] Complies with zone objectives

### **2.2 Secondary Dwelling Provisions (if applicable)**
- [ ] Minimum lot size 450m² (Section 52)
- [ ] Maximum floor area 60m² or as per LEP
- [ ] Total floor area (principal + secondary) within LEP maximum
- [ ] No additional parking required
- [ ] Cannot be subdivided from principal dwelling

### **2.3 Affordable Housing Provisions (if triggered)**
- [ ] Minimum 20% GFA for affordable housing (Section 16)
- [ ] Minimum site area 450m²
- [ ] Landscaped area: 30% minimum (or 35m²/dwelling for social housing)
- [ ] Deep soil zone: 15% of site, 3m minimum dimension
- [ ] Solar access: 70% of dwellings, 3hrs 9am-3pm mid-winter

## **4. PARKING & ACCESS**

### **4.1 Car Parking Requirements**
- [ ] **Dwelling house**: 2 spaces (1 covered)
- [ ] **Secondary dwelling**: No additional required
- [ ] **Visitor parking**: As per DCP
- [ ] **Dimensions**: 5.4m x 2.4m (minimum per space)
- [ ] **Covered parking**: 5.5m x 3.0m (single), 5.5m x 5.5m (double)

### **4.2 Vehicle Access**
- [ ] **Driveway width**: 3m min, 6m max at boundary
- [ ] **Driveway gradient**: 1:4 (25%) maximum
- [ ] **Sight distance**: AS2890.1 compliance
- [ ] **Crossover location**: Clear of services/street trees
